A role is a set of user accounts and/or groups. A role is important because the security of applications is done according to the role. As illustrated on the figure above, the administrator can create, edit, delete or assign users/groups to an existing role.

Create a role

  1. Click on Create. Enter the role name and description.
  2. Click on OK to finish.

Edit a role

You can edit role information and access privileges to shared folders.

  1. Select the role to edit.
  2. Click on Edit and edit the role information.
  3. Click on OK to finish.

Delete a role

  1. Select the role to delete.
  2. Click on Delete and then Yes on the prompt dialogue for confirmation.

Add/Remove Users to a role

To add users to a role:

  1. Select the role and click on Users.
  2. Click on Add.
  3. Select users from the member list, and click on Add. (To make multiple selections, press and hold the "Shift" or "Ctrl" key and click on the desired users.)
  4. Click on OK.

To remove users from a role:

  1. Select the role and click Users.
  2. Select users from the member list.
  3. Click on Remove.
  4. Click on OK.

Add/Remove Groups to a role

To add group members to a role:

  1. Select the group and click on Groups.
  2. Click on Add.
  3. Select group from the group list, and click on Add. (To make multiple selections, press and hold the "Shift" or "Ctrl" key and click on the desired groups.)
  4. Click on OK.

To remove Groups from a role:

  1. Select the role and click on Groups.
  2. Select Groups from the group list.
  3. Click on Remove.
  4. Click on OK.
