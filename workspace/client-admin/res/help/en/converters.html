Associate a converter

  1. Select the dataset in the ComboBox.
  2. Click on Create.

    3. Converter classes panel

    1. Choose a converter that you want to apply

    Field mapping panel

    1. Associate required column to parameters
    2. Fill in required internal parameters
  1. Click on Ok
  2. Click on Save all to save your converters list
    Note:
  1. The dataset associated to this converter must be desactivated and activated back to activate the converter.

Edit a converter

  1. Select a converter in the list
  2. Click on Edit and edit the converter details
  3. Click on Ok
  4. Click on Save all to save your converters list
    Note:
  1. The dataset associated to this converter must be desactivated and activated back to activate the converter.

Delete a converter

  1. Select a converter in the list
  2. Click on delete
  3. Click on Save all to save your converter list.

Delete the converter list

  1. Click on Delete all to completely delete the converter list
