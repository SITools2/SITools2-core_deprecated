Associate a filter

  1. Select the dataset in the ComboBox.
  2. Click on Create.

    3. Converter classes panel

    1. Choose a filter that you want to apply

    Field mapping panel

    1. Associate required column to parameters
    2. Fill in required internal parameters
  1. Click on Ok
  2. Click on Save all to save your filters list
    Note:
  1. The dataset associated to this converter must be desactivated and activated back to activate the filter.

Edit a converter

  1. Select a converter in the list
  2. Click on Edit and edit the filter details
  3. Click on Ok
  4. Click on Save all to save your filters list
    Note:
  1. The dataset associated to this filter must be desactivated and activated back to activate the filter.

Delete a converter

  1. Select a filter in the list
  2. Click on delete
  3. Click on Save all to save your converter list.

Delete the converter list

  1. Click on Delete all to completely delete the converter list
